Bull Terrier

The Bull Terrier was developed from dogs that were used for animal baiting and pit contests, although it has always been kept as a companion. The breed was previously known as the Bull and Terrier, Bull-dog Terrier, White Cavalier, or Hink's Breed, after their creator.
